---What is design thinking? Why are we studying it?
Design thinking is when people are constantly thinking about what their audience wants when they are designing a prouct. We are learning this because this is something people do all the time and it's effective when trying to make something people will like.
---What are the steps of the DEEP design thinking process?
We have to first have to think about what others might like, and to do that we have to get people's opinions on the subject. We ask our targeted audience sets of questions about what things they prefer. Once we have that information we build around it, using their likes and dislikes in mind. After that we make 2 different products and then ask them once again which one they like better through A - B testing.
