We are doing this project based on design thinking because this is something people do all the time and it's effective when trying to make something people will like. Design thinking is when people are constantly thinking about what their audience wants when they are designing a prouct. We have to first have to think about what others might like, and to do that we have to get people's opinions on the subject. We ask our targeted audience sets of questions about what things they prefer. Once we have that information we build around it, using their likes and dislikes in mind. After gathering all the informatio we make 2 different products and then ask them once again which one they like better through A - B testing. In A - B testing we say which on we like more and what their flaws and strengths are. After that we fix what we need to to make it better.

Album Cover

In Hawaii 2014 Viola Krystina and Piper Mavis met in their freshman year in highschool. They were very good friends and then they one day realized that they both had a passion for music. Piper had a really good voice and Viola played the piano. They started making music till one day Brande Avis approached them and asked about their group. He complimented their music and asked if they ever tried to implement irony into their music. He then helped Piper write more music and he learned guitar to play with them. Then in 2016 John Abel sort of came out of nowhere, he started following them to all their plays and was a major fan. One day he asked if he could make the background music for them and they said no. But he insisted and over time, a long long time, they said yes. Back in 2014 Viola and Piper created the band name Island Light because they all met in Hawaii. And they decided to make their first album when Brande joined the band and he suggested the name Temporal Vein because he likes irony. And even though they at first sound like a really cool and soothing band, they actually talk about deep and twisted stuff.
